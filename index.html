HOW TO USE ?

Segment A : Create Tracking

  • Enter your Consignment/ tracking Number
  • Select your Courier Name from the available list
  • GOTO CREATE TRACKING

Segment B : Track Your Created Courier Trackings

  • This Section Will Show all your trackings you made during your session with same browser. The section will include the courier name you used, Your courier tracking ID.
  • Please note: after creating your tracking, it may take sometime to reflect the tracking details in our servers. We are trying hard to help you get your information faster and free of cost, but to avoid misuse of the service and to cop-up with server cost, there maybe a delay in minutes.
  • You will have an option to delete the list or you may track the available list from the previous trackings you made.
  • Once you click on track button on the table, you will be shown with relevant details of the trackings made.
  • Should you have any issue, or should you get stuck with anything, be it technical or user experience related, you should feel free to Contact Us.
